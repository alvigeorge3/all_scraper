How to Store Biscuits to Keep Them Fresh & Crunchy

Proper storage techniques prevent staleness and maintain the original texture that makes biscuits enjoyable to eat.

Airtight Container Storage

Glass jars with tight-fitting lids work exceptionally well for maintaining crispness. Label containers with purchase dates to track freshness periods accurately.

Cool, Dry Location Selection

Store containers away from direct sunlight and heat sources that accelerate deterioration processes. Kitchen cupboards maintain stable temperatures better than countertops near cooking areas. Avoid refrigerator storage unless specifically mentioned on packaging instructions.

Separation by Type

Keep different biscuit varieties in separate containers to prevent flavour mixing and texture changes. Sweet and savoury options require individual storage to maintain their distinct characteristics. Cream-filled varieties need extra protection from temperature fluctuations.

Original Packaging Benefits

Keep unopened packets in original wrappers until consumption time arrives for maximum freshness. Manufacturer packaging provides optimal protection during storage periods before opening. Check expiry dates regularly and use older stock first.
